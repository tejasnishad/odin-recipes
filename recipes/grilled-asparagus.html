Grilled Asparagus

Description

  • Prep time: 10mins
  • Cook time: 5mins
  • Total time:15mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1lb fresh, clean asparagus
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Toss asparagus with olive oil.
  2. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook for three to five minutes on high heat on a grill, or until lightly charred, and tender when pierced with a fork
